What makes our coffee special?

Low quality beans have less flavor, we roast the coffee darker to hide imperfections in the coffee and eke out as much flavor as possible, regardless of what that flavor ends up being. Our coffee quality is beyond the final product served on your cup.

At this point, you may have been acquainted with the words ‘speciality coffee.’ For the uninitiated, speciality coffee is coffee graded above 80, according to the Speciality Coffee Association (SCA). It means that the coffee quality is beyond the final product served on your cup. Its quality springs from the start—the type of coffee beans grown, the region, roasting, and brewing, down to the actual coffee served right under your nose.
